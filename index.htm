ENDING YOUR WIX.COM SUBSCRIPTION

>
REVIEW PAYMENT PERIOD

It's a good idea to check when your subscription period ends, unfortunately wix does not refund any $$ for cancelling early.

As well, make sure you have turned off the auto renew to your wix subscription and if possible remove/cancel your 'premium' subscription.

>
WIX FINE PRINT

Your domain registered with wix will only be taken offline after you officially 'Transfer Away from Wix' and use the authorization code provided to confirm this transfer ...more on this later

After the domain transfer process is confirmed, your official website (under the wix premium subscription) will be taken offline. Unfortunately, wix does not allow users to delete their account, instead defaulting it back to the 'free' version. meaning that your website will remain online at the wix URL accountname.wixsite.com/siteaddress until the end of your subscription period

It is unlikely that anyone will be searching for your website using this wix URL, but i would encourage removing all of your work from your wix account so that is no longer available for wix to host.

>
Archiving YOUR WORK
(10minutes)

Ending your hosting services with wix.com will remove your current website including ALL uploaded files, text, and links.

To avoid losing any work, make sure to create a local copy of all documents, images, and links before making any changes to your wix subscription.

This might look like,

  • Downloading all logo, image, video, and/or audio files and labelling them according to their website organization
  • Copy/pasting accompanying text and externally linked websites and files in a locally saved document
  • Noting any colour codes and font choices you may want to use in the future
  • Consider taking screenshots and screen recordings of your website for reference
