> SAVING YOUR WORK
(10minutes)

Ending your subscription and hosting services with wix.com will remove your current website, including all uploaded files, text, and links.

To avoid losing any work, make sure to create a local copy of all documents, images, and links before making any changes to your wix subscription.This might look like,

  • Downloading all logo, image, video, and/or audio files, taking a moment to organize and label files according to their website organization
  • Copy/pasting accompanying text and externally linked websites and files integrated
  • Consider taking screenshots and/or screen recordings of your website for future reference
